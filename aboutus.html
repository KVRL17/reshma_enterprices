About Us

Reshma Enterprises is a well-established organization that offers a wide range of industrial waste management services, with best eco-friendly practices.

Reshma Enterprises is at the forefront of technology with state of the art machinery to ensure a drum be it steel or plastic that can and does meet the strict standard of today's market place. We are a TNPCB approved company and guarantee a high standard of quality to meet your exact requirements. With our state-of-the-art technology operated by expert workforce, combined with flawless methodology, we are fully equipped to handle thousands of tons of industrial waste tailored to the unique requirements of your organisation. Collection of scrap material from your plant and safe transportation to our premises and segregating them into hazardous and non-hazardous wastes to finally recycling and reconditioning them, we provide a complete solution to managing industrial wastes. At the heart of our existence is a greener safer planet. Because, Reshma Enterprises nurtures the spirit of a pollution-free planet, paving the way for a CLEAN AND GREEN FUTURE . Our products and services include new and reconditioned steel drums, plastic drums, hazardous materials packaging and a full range of container accessories and related products.
